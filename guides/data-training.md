# Data Training

**Our CDN seamlessly integrates with data from any data provider or car manufacturer.**

{% hint style="info" %}
Let us handle the mapping. **Providing your data streamlines the process, ensuring seamless mapping, prioritized image selection, and an ever-evolving service.** Your data fuels the CDN!
{% endhint %}

> To retrieve an image, use our `getImage` API, constructing the URL with query string parameters that specify the image you need.

The IMAGIN.studio CDN requires the following before delivering your image:

> 1. Accurate interpretation of your car data.
> 2. Presence of your specific car model in our system.
> 3. Availability of the requested paint color (including the necessary spray cans).
> 4. Prior production of the car model with the specified paint color.
> 5. Application of your personalized image tailoring settings.

> The quality of our image matching is entirely dependent on the accurate mapping of your customer data to our internal IMAGIN.studio images. Initial data training is crucial for optimal results.

> The platform learns your data from the moment you make your first image request.&#x20;

{% hint style="info" %}
_Tip when getting started: When starting your implementation your user experience may seem quite irregular. If you have an initial car data export for us (CSV file), we can speed up the initial training._
{% endhint %}
