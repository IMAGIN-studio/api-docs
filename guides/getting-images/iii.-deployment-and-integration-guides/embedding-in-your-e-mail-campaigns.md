# 📩 Embedding in your e-mail campaigns

### Optimize Image Display in Your Email Campaigns

## **Image Display Consistency Across Email Clients**

It's important to recognize that different email clients may render images in varying ways. To ensure your visuals always appear as intended, we offer a work around.

## Our API for Flawless Image Delivery&#x20;

Our API integrates seamlessly with your email campaigns. By simply appending `&filetype=jpg` to your image request, you guarantee the API provides images in a format with broad compatibility across email clients.

Example:

https://cdn.imagin.studio/getImage?\&customer={customer-key}\&make=toyota\&modelFamily=yaris\&modelRange=yaris<mark style="color:orange;">\&filetype=jpg</mark>
