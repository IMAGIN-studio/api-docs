# 📐 Selecting Angles

## Selecting Angles: Total Perspective Control

The angle parameter gives you granular control over the camera viewpoint, allowing you to generate everything from static, key marketing shots to high-engagement, seamless 360° spinners.

### The angle Parameter Usage

To request a specific viewpoint, add the `angle` parameter to your `getImage` request:

https://cdn.imagin.studio/getImage?customer={yourcustomerkey}\&make=bmw\&modelFamily=x5<mark style="background-color:yellow;">\&angle=23</mark>

***

#### Building a 360° Spinner

To build a spinner, your application must programmatically cycle through the required angle IDs (e.g., `angle=200` through `angle=231`) for a single vehicle, retrieve all 32 images, and then load them into an interactive image viewer.
