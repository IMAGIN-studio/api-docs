## Comprehensive Model Coverage&#x20;

Our content archive goes beyond current models, providing comprehensive visual coverage dating back to 2016 and earlier for key vehicles. We don't just store one image per model year; our database captures and maps the significant visual variants of each car, including:

* Year-Over-Year Changes: We archive distinct images for facelifts, generational shifts, and subtle visual updates across different model years (e.g., specific images for the 2018 vs. 2019 version of the same vehicle).
* Trim and Version Specificity: The API can accurately retrieve the specific body style or trim level your customer selects, eliminating the confusion caused by generic or inaccurate imagery.

This means you can trust the IMAGIN.studio API to provide the precise, historically accurate visual for every vehicle in your inventory, regardless of its age or specification.
