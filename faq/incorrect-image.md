# Incorrect image

<table><thead><tr><th width="240">Question</th><th>Answer</th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>What if the image is incorrect or not the one I expect?</td><td><p>Our system works hard to find the best match for your image requests, even if your data is incomplete or has minor inaccuracies. If a perfect match isn't immediately available, you'll first see an initial vehicle that closely resembles what you're looking for. Our CDN then uses machine learning to analyze your request and identify the exact match, and this displayed image will automatically update once the correct one is found. This means you'll always have a visual to work with.</p><p>Our CDN uses this data to forecast your needs and shift priority for our modeling team. This means the more a specific missing vehicle or trim is requested, the higher its priority becomes for creation.</p><p>In the rare event that your data doesn't match any existing images at all—perhaps due to significant inaccuracies or an extremely uncommon vehicle model—you might see a placeholder image (a vehicle covered by a blanket). If this occurs, please reach out to us with the details, and we'll be happy to help.</p><p>Our CDN continuously uses machine learning to learn from new data, optimizing image delivery and improving accuracy with every request over time.</p></td></tr></tbody></table>
